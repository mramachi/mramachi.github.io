Features

Different functions your citizens can use to make their lives easier.

Report issues

Littering, graffiti or loose bricks on the road. Your citizens now have an easy way to report any issue they are facing during their day! They can also look through the issues submitted by other citizens to upvote or comment on them. That way you can prioritize the issues.

Receive Feedback

Have one place where you can gather feedback from any citizen.

Chatbot

Save time and resources by giving your citizens an easy way to find what they need on your website through a chatbot.

Update your citizens on your plans

Through our newsfeed section, your citizens can easily get up to date with everything you post on the CityShaper website or on any of your social media platforms.

Plan your resources efficiently

Through our application, you can have a quick overview on which problems need your priority and how you can efficiently plan your resources.

Dashboard and Analytics

Get an immediate overview of everything that is happening regarding your citizens. Make sure your resources are effiently distributed. You can also export reports in CSV or any other format you need.
